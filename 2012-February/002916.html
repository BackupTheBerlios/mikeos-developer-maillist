G'day all,
I created a level editor for my upcoming game release, for those
of you who don't read my blog, here the link to my idea:
http://unix-like.tumblr.com/post/17759727321/rts-game
I'll need levels to test it on, so I made the level editor first.

Basically it's a modified version of ASCII Artist, that lets you put
'entities' or units on top of the 'picture'. Press enter to create
and edit the properties of units (max 200). There's plenty of
good stuff in the menu.

Get it here:
http://code.google.com/p/mikebasic-applications/downloads/detail?name=lvledit.bas&can=2&q=

Enjoy, and let me know if you make any good maps/levels.

Joshua,
http://code.google.com/p/mikebasic-applications/
