Do you really need that many pixels?
As far as I can tell VGA mode 12h is 640x480x16 and you have to mess around
with "planes" and stuff like that to use it.
I think a far better choice would be mode 13h.
This mode is 320x200x256, and has a single byte of memory in the video
segment for each pixel It's really easy to use, you could have
your video driver written in ten minutes.
