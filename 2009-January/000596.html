To be honest you can't generate truly random numbers (at least in the  
mathematical sense) on a PC. You use a pseudo-random number generator  
which produces a number between two values which is very unlikely to  
be the same as the next number - and that is generally good enough  
from a developer point of view. :-)

The Fibonacci approach was used in Civilization for those of you  
who've played that, and I think Matlab used to use it for it's rand()  
function. It's definitely food for thought, anyway!
