On Tue, 24 Nov 2009 22:07:13 +0000
M Saunders <okachi at gmail.com> wrote:
> Should I add IN and OUT instructions to the BASIC
> interpreter to read and write to ports?

My idea is to wrap the accessing of a parallel port in PAROUT and PARIN
functions.

Example:

	PAROUT LPT1 , 12       <-- write 12 to the port
	PARIN LPT1, $A         <-- read port and store in A variable.

This basic interpreter is cool by the way, great job :)
