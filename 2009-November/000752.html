>
> Unless I've got something else fundementally wrong, even forcing es
> back to the segment with a constant doesn't seem to work. Without going
> to great legths for some funny debugging routines, do you know of any
> debugger extensions to either qemu or bochs that I can use?

Not sure if you know this already, but in QEMU you can switch to a
monitor mode and look at registers etc. When I'm in your position, I
put 'jmp $' into the code at the stage where things go wrong, and then
hit Ctrl+Alt+2 in QEMU to switch to the monitor.

You can then type 'print $eip' to print the instruction pointer, for
instance. There's other stuff it can do like pause and resume
execution, so that might help you to narrow down where things go
wrong.

Good luck!
