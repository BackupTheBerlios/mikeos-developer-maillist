Hello Mike & all,

I'm just guessing here but flashnul probably isn't doing anything special, in fact, rawwrite could do the image copy if he could see a target drive other than floppy, so linux boxes may not even need flashnul... not sure though. Could be true that partcopy might even do it but it is safer to know for sure where the write is going( which flashnul gives an indication of  ) before commiting and a little easier to say "write all this to my usb stick, here is the data" than giving params for start point and length in hex to partcopy, and possibly sending it to the wrong drive. I do believe it would be wise for some other folks to test booting MikeOS from usb, in case it's just a quirk in certain bioses, by the way, I have Legacy USB Support enabled in my bios, which may or may not make a difference, haven't tested with different settings yet.
