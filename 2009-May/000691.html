Got a message today from a programmer looking to write MikeOS stuff in
FASM. I did some rummaging around and converted the sample hello.asm
and mikedev.inc to FASM format. Very simple stuff:

1) 'BITS 16' in NASM becomes 'use16' in FASM
2) '%INCLUDE' in NASM becomes 'include' in FASM
3) '%DEFINE' in NASM becomes 'define' in FASM

I've only tried the simple hello.asm program; there may be cases where
more stuff needs to be changed. But I just thought I'd post this for
the benefit of any FASM fans :-)
