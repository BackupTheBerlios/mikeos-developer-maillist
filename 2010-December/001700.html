> If so, are there (I hope) BIOS interrupts for grabbing and writing data
> to the USB?

No.

STANDARD BIOS uses Interrupts 00h to 1Fh, and can possibly include int 4Fh.

USB socket I/O doesn`t exist here BUT emulates KB and Boot systems down
to LEGACY levels, this icludes original I/O addresses and to PS/2 I/O also;
the Mouse can also be supported, down to PS/2 levels.
