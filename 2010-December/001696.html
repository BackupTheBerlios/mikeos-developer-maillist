You don`t need interrupt 10 for much of Video Mode 3, (CGA text mode.

The default page starts at absolute address 0xB8000 and every EVEN address is the character
and every ODD address is the colour, both are byte values, and as the screen contains 2000 characters
linearly related then bit is easy to produce XY values of your own without the need of many
INT 10h functions.

Plus on slow machines it would be faster too.

Take a look at the TESTCARD.ZIP archive for a full screen example.
I`m sure the assembly code is in there.
