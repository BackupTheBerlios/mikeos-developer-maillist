Hi royce_33...

Sorry to disappoint you here matey but "INT 20" is an MS-DOS interrupt and
doesn`t exist in BIOS.

You are also compiling as though it is a .COM file.

It is used as an obsolete "clean" termination routine for MS-DOS; replaced by
"INT 21" function 4Ch.
