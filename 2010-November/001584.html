complicated work. A lot of folks think they can just write one driver
for a single device but they soon get lost in types of host
controllers, device enumeration, descriptors, parsing data packets
etc. It would be way bigger and more complex than the rest of MikeOS
combined -- again, detracting from the original goal of the project.
