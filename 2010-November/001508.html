> How did you guys create the image file?

The easy way. ;o)
Windows(XP), USB floppy disk drive, blank diskette, and RAWRITE.EXE.
The USB drive defaults to A: so behaves like an ancient internal 3.5 inch A: drive.

Never fails.

No CLI required.
(BTW also works on my multi-core Vista tool too.)
