Exactly why I mentioned the LM3915.
Data sheet here   http://www.national.com/ds/LM/LM3915.pdf

Just remember that the outputs of the LM3915 are active low (i.e. they sink current).
Keep that in mind when interfacing to the KB.
Also don't forget to set the LM3915 in dot mode.
