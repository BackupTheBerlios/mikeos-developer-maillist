Bazza,

I suspect the reason your keyboard code from the 8088 days does not work any more
is due to the fact that they used XT keyboards where as the new ones are PS/2 as you know.
I have no idea which ports were used for the XT and what the protocol was, but
with PS/2 all is done via ports 60h and 64h.

As far as IrDA is concerned, like you say it's not a very popular port but a lot of desktop mobo's
do have the port but just need the infra-red transceiver to be plugged in.
Luckily both my laptops do have IrDA so I will use the one with XP and the other for MikeOS.

Will keep you posted.
