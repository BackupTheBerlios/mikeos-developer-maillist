Hi all,

Just gave 4.2.b2 a go on a Toshiba Satellite Laptop.

1) Music keyboard does not produce
  any sounds.Most new PC's no longer have a
  on board speaker so I guess it's only for
  older hardware.

2) After terminating calc.bas, the screen stays
   blank until a key is pressed which then goes
  back to the editor.
  This could be confusing to someone, thinking
  it has crashed.

3) Calc.bas crashes after loading a file.

4) Memedit is very nice, but could be made
 more user friendly by having the ASCII char
 on the right also highlighted same as the
 corresponding HEX byte on the left.

  I would also prefer to have the line numbers
  and offsets in hex as opposed to decimal.

My 2 cents worth.
Paulo.
