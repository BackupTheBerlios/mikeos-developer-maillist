For 4.3 I plan to include a READ statement which lets you read numeric
values from labels, to avoid repetitive POKEs. For instance, you will
be able to do


FOR B = 40000 TO 40010
    READ A mydata
    POKE A B
NEXT B

mydata:
11 56 201 3 104 56 234 9 77 19


Every READ operation will increment the pointer in the label. It
should make some BASIC programs a bit smaller and neater!
