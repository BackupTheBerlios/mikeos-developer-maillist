I release another update to MikeTron

Added a stop bonus which makes the game pause until you press a
direction key (WASD).
Added a bonus that makes five more bonuses occur. These are very rare (1/20).
Made bombs do a cool explosion effect.
Changed bonus quote at game start.
You now get five more bonuses for every 100 point (stops after 1000).
