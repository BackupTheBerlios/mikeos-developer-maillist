Hi Mike,

I'm "showing you the code" :-)  for
AND, XOR and OR logical functions
added to MikeBASIC.

The syntax I have used is the same as for
C/C++ where:

& = logical AND
^ = logical XOR
| = logical OR

I haven't bothered with a NOT function
as it can be easily done as follows:
(assuming 16 bits)

A = 65535 - B which is the same as
A = ! B

I have attached the modified Basic.asm
 and have added comments in this format:
(so they will be easy to find)

;<--change_230911--><0001>
;<--change_230911--><0002>
;<--change_230911--><0003>
;<--change_230911--><0004>

etc etc

Please let me know what you think.
Cheers,
Paulo.
