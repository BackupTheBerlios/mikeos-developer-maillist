I decided to give the game MikeTron in EXAMPLE.BAS a little upgrade...
Changes I've made to MikeTron:

 Going off the side of the screen makes you go to the other side unharmed.
 Added three bonuses generated in random locations on the screen.
 Bonus one makes you not leave a trail as you move.
 Bonus two makes an explosion destroying all blocks in a 5x10 radius.
 Bonus three makes you invisible and pass through all blocks.
 Vertical speed is half of horizontal speed to make up for character height.
 Keys are now WASD.
 You can press escape to exit the game at any time.
 Trail character has been changed.
 Changed startup message.

I am happy with the finished product but maybe I’ll update it again sometime.
