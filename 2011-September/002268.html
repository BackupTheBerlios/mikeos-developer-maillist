> 2. EDIT cuts off text on BAS files longer than 80 chars (ascii art + print function) perhaps could use horizontal scroll?

Sure could, if anyone wants to implement it! It's not easy -- you've
got two ways of doing it. You could show just part of the line and let
the user scroll through it, or you could show the whole line wrapped
as necessary.

In the first scenario you've got to be especially careful in terms of
matching the on-screen position with the byte in memory that you're
editing; in the second, the lines shown on the screen won't
necessarily match the lines in the file. It gets complicated, hence
why I didn't implement it. And BASIC programs shouldn't be that wide
anyway :-)

But if anyone wants to write the code, good luck!
