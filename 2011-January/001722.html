> 1. I'm having problem using grub. I get the following error messages when

> Error 13: Invalid or unsupported executable format

You didn't tell us ANYTHING about your kernel, which makes it
extremely hard to help. Don't be so vague. In what format is your
kernel (a.out? ELF? Flat binary?)

Do you realise that GRUB boots kernels in protected mode? I'm assuming
your kernel is in real mode from other posts you've made. In which
case why are you using GRUB?

And if it's a flat binary for protected mode execution, you should
include a multiboot header like this:
http://f.osdev.org/viewtopic.php?f=1&t=21260

You can't just throw GRUB onto a floppy and expect it to work. Read
the documentation: http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/manual/html_node/
