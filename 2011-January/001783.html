Hello,

I don't know the detail of Windows7/GnuWin32, but I hope these hints
will solve your problem.

	- try with new Dev86 package, at least Dev86-0.16.17.
	  (next libmikeos is going to use Dev86-0.16.18)
	- use GNU Make, not BSD make
	- care with space character in file/directory name,
	  such as "My Documents"
