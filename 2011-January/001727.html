1) Reply to your OWN thread. Stop making new ones each time. It messes
up the archives and is annoying for subscribers who don't want to

2) You can't just convert some bytes in the disk table. FAT32 is a
different filesystem to FAT12. Read ALL of this document:

http://www.scribd.com/doc/2187273/FAT32-File-System-Specification

Then you'll understand what you're dealing with. Then bear in mind
that you'll need to write a driver for the different device (USB key
etc.) you're trying to handle. As you're still a beginner with a
simple real mode OS, this will take you several months (at least).

Muzaffar, post no more messages without doing lots of research
YOURSELF first. Stop just coming up with random ideas and asking the
list how to do it.
