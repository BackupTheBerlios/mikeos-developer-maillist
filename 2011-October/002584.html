MUNCHER!
VERSION = 1.0
BY: Justin Tokarchuk
-----------------
Muncher is an action game. The role that you play is of a '#' sign, that
loves to munch apples (@). Beware though! If you munch too many apples,
the world will create obstacles for you!
Controls: W S A D (Up, Down, Left, Right)
The more you eat, the higher your score! See if you can roll over the
scoreboard! (65535)

--

Test it out and let me know if you guys like it!
