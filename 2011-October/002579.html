Mike,

I know you most likely won't be able
to read this for a few days but,
I think I'm getting closer to solving
the mystery about images and isos
working on some machines and not others.

I suspect it has something to do with CHS versus
LBA disk access.
Once we use FAT16 with bigger "disks"
then one has to use INT13h extended functions
which make use of LBA.
How "strict" a specific machine is about this is
controlled by it's BIOS.

The reason the iso worked for both of us could be down
to the BIOS implementation of our VMs

I also notice that the BPB on the iso
is different to the original one in the ima.
This could have something with compensating for the
fact that it's running on a CD and is modified by
iso making app.
See attached ScreenShot.

Mike Gonta what do you say?
