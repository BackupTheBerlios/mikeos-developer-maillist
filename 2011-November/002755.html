On Sat, 12 Nov 2011 13:25:16 +0000, M Saunders wrote:
> Er, and your BASIC.BAS has a weird arrangement of non-ASCII 
> characters
> under the "command:" part at the start. I don't know if this is some
> sort of trick you're using, but it's not officially supported by the
> OS BASIC interpreter!
It shows up in Notepad++ as a bunch of NUL characters. I'm not quite 
sure what benefit this has vs. putting it at some location in memory 
followed by a return and gosub'ing to it. People's text editors could 
mess up on the NULs in BASIC.BAS and render it unusable. In fact, it's 
entirely possible that the MikeOS text editor may do this itself!

It may be wise to change those NULs to spaces to prevent text editors 
from rendering your code FUBAR.
