<Justin at bakercomputers.com> wrote:
> Not to be a pain, but can someone point me in the right direction to fix this issue? I'm novice with ASM and would like to use these opportunities to get better.

If you're a novice then trying to hunt down a picky little bug in a
complex piece of software is NOT a good place to start. Many, many
people have come to MikeOS with little asm experience, tried to do
something big and given up, so I recommend starting small and working
your way up. EDIT is one of the most complex bits of code in MikeOS so
steer clear until you feel very comfortable with asm.

A good way to learn, however, is to take very simple DOS games that
have source code (especially games that compile to .COM executables)
and port them to MikeOS. The MikeOS API is very well documented so you
don't need to ask lots of questions. Good luck!
