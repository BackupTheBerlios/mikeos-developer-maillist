>As regards detecting the capacity of a hard drive, I'm sure there is an ATA 
>command for that.
>Let me check the specs and I will get back to you.

Yes there is, it's the Identify Drive (ECh) command and returns 256 words of 
very useful information about the drive.
To check the capacity of the drive look at words 60 - 61 (4 bytes) which reports 
the total number of sectors addressable in LBA mode.
Each sector is 512 bytes as usual.
