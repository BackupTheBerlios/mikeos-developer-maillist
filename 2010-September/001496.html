Hi Paulo,

> Since you are in the process of upgrading and revamping MikeOS,
> what would be involved in adding FAT16 support?

I think the current FAT12 implementation could be modified for FAT16.
In many ways, FAT16 is much simpler to work with as you're dealing
with two bytes at a time (rather than a byte and a nibble from
another).

However, we'd need a new bootloader, support for multiple disk
geometries etc. and other things I have no clue about. It's one of
those: good idea, but let's have some code first! type of things.
