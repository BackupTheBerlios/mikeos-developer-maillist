>ANIMATE.BAS - Changing the speeds from low to high - I found on that ther was a massive
>difference in animation speed from the setup speed (extremely fast) and the slower speed
>below that.
Not a bug. There is no "fast" and "slow", simple different delays
between drawing,
i.e. 0s 0.1s 0.2s 0.4s 0.8s 1.6s. When you press the 'Q' key the delay
(if any) halfs,
when you press 'E' it doubles. You can do this as much as you want.
The default is no time at all, when the drawing effect is most apparent.
This means it will draw it as fast as it can, which can seem extreamly
fast if you have
a decent machine. At the time I wrote it we could not measure any
smaller intervals
than 0.1 seconds. Actually, we can now measure 0.05 seconds (with
"TIMER" keyword), but
I'm not sure if it's really worth implementing.
