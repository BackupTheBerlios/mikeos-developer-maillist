G'day all,
I've created a cool new space themed game, called "Cosmic Flight".
In this game you guide a small space craft through a dangerous galaxy.

The aim of the game is to collect green items to get as much energy as
possible and avoid red items, which drain your energy.
As you progress the game becomes much harder, making danger everywhere
and energy few and far between.

Plus you can fire lots of cools laser weapons that get better with your energy.

Controls are 'A' and 'D' for movement and space to fire.

I wrote the whole thing in MikeOS BASIC using the OS text editor (might need
page up/down) and it has plenty of comments so you can understand the workings.

Although you can play it in an emulator, I would recommend playing it on real
hardware because in my experience, in a emulator you get a small screen,
no PC speaker sound and poor random number seeds that kind of spoil the game.

I managed to score 20100 for my highest energy, see if you can beat me!

Get it here:
http://code.google.com/p/mikebasic-applications/downloads/detail?name=cosmicflight-2.0.0.tar.gz&can=2&q=
