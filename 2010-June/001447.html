Hi Matej,

Your os_exponentation routine has two errors:

1) By doing a pop AX at the end, you over write part of the result (the other part being in DX).

2) Your routine does not cater for exponent being a zero.
    For example: 2 ^ 0 = 1 , in fact any number to the exponent zero is equal to one .
    This is a perfectly legal mathematical expression.

Paulo.
