Hi Bazza,

Is there any reason you include an INT 9h after updating 0040:0017h?
The reason I ask is the keyboard controller will automatically update
the led status once you write to that location.

i.e.

push ds
mov dx,0040h
mov ds,dx
mov al,40h  ; 40h for caps lock    20h for num lock   and   10h for scroll lock
mov [0017h],al
pop ds

No INT 9h required so it will shave off another byte or two from your code.

Paulo.
