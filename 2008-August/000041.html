Hmm... I've tried it on a few physical devices.

On an Eee PC 900 (USB floppy drive) - Most of the OS works but you can get
sluggish performance. The 'mouse.bin' module loads but then terminates
straight away (you don't get the chance to see if the touchpad works) -
perhaps this is a debugging measure, as it wasn't designed for trackpad
mice!

Likewise a similar thing happens on an old Toshiba satellite (You know the
old grey ones with the green sponge in the middle of the keyboard as a
mouse?). The mouse.bin module mostly works, but I do sometimes get some
bizarre co-ordinate addresses in the bottom-left.

It seems to work perfectly with my desktop machine though (with a PS/2
mouse). though USB  mouse performance tends to be a bit more erratic...!
