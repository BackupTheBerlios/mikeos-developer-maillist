Labels just represent the position in the code -- the assembler
translates them to a number. If you've got

mylol:
  db 'LOL', 0

in your code, then 'mylol' is translated to 0x346A or whatever. If you
add stuff before 'mylol', then its position increases. But it's just
shorthand for a number.
