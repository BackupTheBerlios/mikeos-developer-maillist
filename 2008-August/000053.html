>
>  Stable, amazing. I guess now my job is either multitasking

OK, but bear in mind that it'll involve a major architectural change
to the OS! You will need to allocate programs into their own segments,
and transform the current os_ call system into interrupts (ie
refactoring the 40+ system calls) for it to work effectively.

> or a TCP stack.

And for that you'll need to understand everything here:

http://www.faqs.org/rfcs/rfc793.html

Nice bedtime reading! Writing a TCP/IP from stack is a Herculean
effort, so porting one from the likes of Contiki would be slightly
simpler. But you'd still have to understand the ins-and-outs of the
above document, and then code a bunch of Ethernet drivers (or try for
SLIP over the serial port).

I'm not saying don't go for it, but bear in mind the vast amount of
work those things will take :-)
