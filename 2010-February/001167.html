Hi Mike,

> PRINT 12 "foobar"

Good idea.
I assume you will do a bit check as per my picture in the previous post so as to allow for background colour, blinking, etc.

For example:

PRINT 12 "foobar"    prints foobar in colour 12 (pinkish red) on black background.
PRINT 140 "foobar"   prints the same as above but the text flashes (128 + colour).
PRINT 142 "foobar"   prints foobar in same colour as above but on a dark green background and flashing.

For asm, I assume you would add the os_print_colour_string.


Paulo.
