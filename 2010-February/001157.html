Hi Mike,

> Finer control in os_pause

That would be great, but keep in mind the resolution of the 18.2mSec timer.
Anything smaller (i.e. down to a few hundred microseconds) one would have to "sync"
to the vga controller by detecting the start of a new line.
In screen 13h and 03h, one line takes 900 clocks at 28.322MHz which equates to 32 microsenconds (give or take a couple).

For even finer resolution down to a few microseconds you could use the old out 80h, 0 trick so beloved by linux coders 
which gives very close to 1 microsecond and is processor speed independent.

> make the program menu scrollable

That would be very nice as now the text just over runs.
I suspect that you would also have to allocate a bigger buffer for files as when one
adds more than 18 files to the image it just hangs.
The culprits seem to be os_get_file_list and os_file_exists as when I modified the kernel and took out
any references to these then made the kernel load autorun.bin I could have lots and lots of files (tried it with 35).
