Hi again,

Are you sure that the exe you are referring to is not just a self extracting zip archive and when you run it you will get 2 files,
the flashing utility and the rom/bin file ?

Why not look at the exe in a hex editor?
If it's a self extracting zip archive, you will see some ascii characters which will be the names of the files the archive will produce when run.

A word of warning, proceed with caution because if you accidentally flash your bios and something goes wrong you will have
a very expensive paper weight.
