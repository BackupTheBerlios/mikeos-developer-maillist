Hi nascent mind...

> I meant the BIOS code. Is there any way I can extract it out from the ROM?
> My machine's flasher comes with an installer and the os is embedded in it.

There are many ways.
1) Use MS DEBUG.EXE again and create "splitz" of 32KB in size and "joinz" to join
them up again. I might add this is all I use as it is good enough for me.
2) Use a debugger/disassembler say AD86 and extract the ROM area(s) as a file.
3) Try a HEX viewer/editer and saver.

This is heavy serious reading, I`ve already got it along with around 20+
other PDFs from AMD about everything to do with the AMD 64 bit architecture.
All hyper-serious reading and most I don`t understand... ;o)
