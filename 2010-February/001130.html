Coreboot has certain advantages but it's also the kind of thing that could drive you mad when a machine's hardware misbehaves.
I see no advantages to using it with MikeOS as it boots so quickly anyway.
Yes it's also great for say internet kiosks (using linux) as the OS is basically read only which makes the threat of a virus completely non-existent.
Once again no advantage with MikeOS  as when you boot it from a cd  it's read only as well.
