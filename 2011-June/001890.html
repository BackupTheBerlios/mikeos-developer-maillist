Looks like this has been solved too. Mike Gonta talked to Mike
Chambers who makes the emulator, and apparently its BIOS doesn't
support the "get enhanced keystroke" interrupt (function 10h on int
16h) which MikeOS uses.

Mike Chambers has added a quick hack fix for now, which appears to
show the menu properly. He says his emulator is actually 80186
compatible too.
