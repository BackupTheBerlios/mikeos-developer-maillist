Quick reply as I am off to work...
Study the BIOS interrupt vectors thoroughly because this is in 16 bit Segment/Offset
and is all you have to play with for an OS like MikeOS.
You are looking at drivers for your needs and the ability to access 32, (64), bit addresses
for more modern HW. Very old sound cards and Single Speed CD R/W players COULD be
accessed inside the 20 bit address boundary but this was 20+ years ago.
