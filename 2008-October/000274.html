On 10/5/08, Troy Martin <blasterman95 at gmail.com> wrote:
>
> I don't see why line 27 jumps out of range in edit.asm... it's attached.

Presumably because it's attempting a short jump, which is limited to
-128/+127 bytes back or forward? Change that to 'je near help' and it
should work. Read up on jump instructions:
http://faydoc.tripod.com/cpu/jmp.htm

Also, I really wouldn't bother playing around with help dialogs and
such at this stage. That's all sugar that can be added right at the
end. Currently your program still isn't an editor (in that you can't
go back and actually edit what you've inputted). That, along with
multi-line handling, is infinitely more important -- otherwise you're
choosing the type of wallpaper for your house when the foundations
haven't been built.

Once you've got multi-line editing in place, we have something serious
to look at!
