Cool. Make sure you understand all of this first:

http://mikeos.berlios.de/handbook.html#asmprimer

A string is just a series of bytes in RAM. You want to cmp the three
extension cars to see if they match .BIN, otherwise jump out (this all
happens at runtime -- you can't use IF and ELSE as they're for the
preprocessor, as I understand it).
