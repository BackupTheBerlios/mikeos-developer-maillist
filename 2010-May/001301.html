Bazza,

> how do I write to the KB in MikeBASIC?

For the "output", you could either add a function/routine to the source and recompile
or you could write directly to the keyboard controller hanging either on the real
PS2 port on the emulated one via USB.

PEEK and POKE would work nicely.

The keyboards LEDs are mapped to one of the registers in the keyboard controller.
So by simply setting those bits on or off, the leds would either be on or off.
I've got all the info/addresses of the keyboard controller IC somewhere.
I could always look for it and email it to you if you like.

For the "input" you could use GETKEY.

Paulo.
