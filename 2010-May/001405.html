It is strange, 'cause as you say SOUND 1000 1 also uses the same delay routine.

Are you sure that it's not happening closer to 60 minutes?
There are 18 ticks per second and the DX register can hold a value of up to 65535.
Therefore 65536/18 = 3640.83 seconds or aprox. 60 minutes.

If this is the case, then the code I posted last night will fix it.
Alternatively why not try this:

beepit:

sound 1000 1
sound 400 2
goto beepit

Theoretically you should now have 1KHz for 1 second followed by 400Hz for 2 seconds, ad nauseam.
Now if both frequencies have no glitches then the problem is elsewhere.
