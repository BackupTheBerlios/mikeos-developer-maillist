Mike,

I've tried dd --filter=removable and it seems to work, at least there are no error messages.

Please note that there is a mistake in my step-by-step post.
Step 4 should read (note that I have now added the filter option):

dd --filter=removable if=c:\mikeos\mikeos41.ima of=\\?\Device\Harddisk1\Partition0

Obviously change if=c:\.... to whatever image you want to put on the flash drive
and change of=\\?\Device\..... to suit your flash drive by first running dd --list to get all the drives info.


If everything goes to plan, dd will print to stdout:

2880+0 records in
2880+0 records out

Hope it helps.

Paulo.
