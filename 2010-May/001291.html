Nicely done.

Why not use the CAPSLOCK, NUMLOCK and SCROLL LOCK LED signals
as CLOCK,DATA, and STROBE signals feeding one or more shift registers.
In this way you can have as many TTL/CMOS outputs as you like.

The 74HC4094 would be perfect.
See http://www.st.com/stonline/books/pdf/docs/1972.pdf

For inputs, you could use transistors connected to the F1 to F12 keys as these are hardly ever used in MikeOS.
