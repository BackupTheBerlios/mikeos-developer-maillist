Paulo,

It shouldn't pose too many problems I hope:

1) Burn and boot Ubuntu ISO
2) Download MikeOS zip file, right-click to extract
3) Copy mikeos.flp to the desktop
4) Insert USB key, right-click its icon on the desktop and chose Safely Remove
5) Go to Applications > Accessories > Terminal, enter 'cd Desktop'
6) Follow the steps in: http://mikeos.berlios.de/handbook-user.html#writing

It would be great to have a sure-fire way of getting this to work on
Windows though...
