The entry level CPU suggested for MikeOS is a 386.
I've never come across a 386 with a clock speed of less than 33MHz.

But even on a 286 8MHz machine, the time taken to execute one opcode which takes one machine cycle is 125 nanoseconds.
So using my original example of 200 clocks it's still only 25 microseconds.
I doubt very much anyone will get upset that their delay took 110,025 milliseconds as opposed to 110 milliseconds.
