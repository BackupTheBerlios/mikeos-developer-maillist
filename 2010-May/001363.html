Not as simple as putting the mov ah, 86h inside the loop as some BIOSes
don't have INT1Ah sub-function 0, full stop.
On others the INT 1Ah has been "reclaimed" for other uses.

So to be 100% sure, the best is probably to move away from INT 1Ah altogether.
