2013/10/27 Joshua Beck <mikeosdeveloper at gmail.com>

> Welcome new person, safe travels through the MikeOS lands.
>
> >there are two GUI's?
> The grey interface with the mouse is a really old interface that was
> testing mouse integration, but it didn't work properly. That's about
> as much as I know. The main interface (actually a TUI) that is
> included on all recent MikeOS versions is the colourful keyboard
> interface. You will find that if you download the latest MikeOS
> version off the home page there will only have one interface not two.
>
> A lot of people have tried since then to fix the graphical/mouse
> interface but none have yet succeed.
>
> BTW To change your "name" in Gmail:
>  - Click on the setting icon in the top right hand corner
>  - Choose the 'settings' option.
>  - Switch to the accounts tab
>  - Find your current mail address in the "Send mail as: " category.
>  - Locate the 'edit info' button to the right of your address and click it.
>  - Change your name.
